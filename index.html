Player Bio

Savon Cameron is a starter for T.F. North High School on the Sophmore team. As a starter he plays the 3 and/or 4 position as a Small Forward/Power Forward. He has great hands with the ability to defend all position 1 through 5. He has great on the court vision and a excellent basketball IQ.


His primary role on the team is to defend and disrupt all traffic coming to the paint. With these great hand abilities, it allows him to block defenders shots and grab boards when the opponents miss a shot. He also has the ability to score and take his opponent off the dribble.

TF North High School

T.F. North High School dates back to 1917 when Wentworth High School was organized and housed only Calumet City students. On April 12, 1924, the Thornton Fractional Township District was organized, and Thornton Fractional Township High School opened in March of 1926. On December 20, 1933, Thornton Fractional Township High School was destroyed by fire. The main part of the present building was rebuilt on the same site and reopened on February 7, 1936.
